Carriage Tour of Historic Charleston
8 Guignard Street, Charleston, SC

Sit in the lap of luxury as you cruise the streets of Charleston in style and comfort, on an amazing carriage ride. Expert guidance, professionalism and knowledge await your arrival to explore this iconic destination.

Palmetto Carriage Works is the oldest carriage tour company in Charleston and has developed the greatest carriage experience in the city! A Palmetto Carriage tour offers a blend of knowledge, wit, humor and history and is exciting and ever changing, a seamless fusion of 19th century conveyance in a 21st century city.

On this hour long tour, you will ride a carriage through the Historic District of Charleston passing beautiful antebellum homes, garden, mansions, and churches while hearing the history of the "Holy City." All tours start in the Big Red Barn where you will get an opportunity to visit with the company's four legged employees while they take their breaks. You will then board the carriage and tour 30-40 blocks of the city and return back to the barn. A Palmetto Carriage Works tour is a must do while visiting Charleston.
